K2

K2 is a tactical board game that simulates the challenges of climbing the world's second-highest mountain. Players take on the roles of climbers, aiming to reach the summit of K2 while managing their resources, stamina, and risks posed by the harsh environment.

The game features a dual-layer mechanic where players must carefully plan their movements across the mountain terrain, dealing with changing weather conditions and the need for oxygen. Each climber has unique abilities, allowing for different strategies and approaches to reach the peak.

Players compete to see who can get the highest climber to the summit and safely back down, as points are awarded based on their progress and survival. With its blend of strategy, risk management, and competitive elements, K2 offers an engaging experience for those who enjoy adventure-themed games and tactical decision-making.
