Bandido

Bandido is a cooperative, puzzle-style card game where players work together to stop a prisoner from escaping through a series of tunnels. The game begins with a starting card showing the bandido surrounded by multiple tunnel exits. Players take turns drawing and playing cards, each depicting new tunnel sections, in an attempt to block all possible escape routes before the deck runs out.

The challenge comes from coordinating moves with other players, as each card can either help block an exit or open new paths. Since the tunnels become more complex as the game progresses, players must carefully plan their moves and communicate to avoid letting the bandido escape.

Bandido is quick, easy to learn, and ideal for players looking for a fast-paced, cooperative puzzle game with high replayability.
