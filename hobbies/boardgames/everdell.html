Everdell

Everdell is a charming worker placement and tableau-building game set in a whimsical forest filled with cute critters and vibrant environments. Players take on the roles of woodland creatures trying to build their own cities over the course of four seasons.

In each turn, players place workers to gather resources, draw cards, and build structures or recruit critters to their tableau. The game features beautiful artwork and components, including a 3D tree that serves as a central hub for resource management.

Strategically, players must balance resource collection with building actions, taking advantage of the unique abilities of critters and structures to maximize their points. With its engaging gameplay, thematic depth, and stunning visuals, Everdell offers a delightful experience for both casual and seasoned gamers, encouraging strategic planning and clever combinations.
