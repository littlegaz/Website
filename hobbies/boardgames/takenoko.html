Takenoko

Takenoko is a charming board game where players take on the roles of gardeners caring for a giant panda and cultivating a beautiful bamboo garden for the Emperor of Japan. The game combines resource management, tile placement, and set collection in a vibrant, whimsical setting.

Players take turns performing actions to grow bamboo, irrigate the garden, and complete objectives involving the panda's eating habits and the growth of bamboo. Each turn offers players choices on how to expand their plots, manage resources, and fulfill tasks to score points.
