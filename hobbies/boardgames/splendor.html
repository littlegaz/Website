Splendor

Splendor is a captivating engine-building game where players assume the roles of Renaissance merchants aiming to acquire wealth and prestige through the collection of precious gems and development of valuable cards.

Players take turns collecting gem tokens, purchasing development cards, and gaining points through strategic investments. Each card provides resources, which can lead to more powerful cards and bonuses, creating a chain of actions that enhances gameplay.

The game is known for its simple rules and deep strategic options, allowing players to develop their strategies while adapting to the moves of their opponents. With its elegant components and engaging mechanics, Splendor offers a balanced mix of luck and skill, making it a favorite for both casual players and seasoned gamers.
