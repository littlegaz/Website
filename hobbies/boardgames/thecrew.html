The Crew

The Crew: Deep Sea Mission is a cooperative trick-taking card game where players embark on an underwater adventure, working together to complete a series of challenging missions. Building on the success of its predecessor, The Crew, this game introduces new mechanics and an engaging aquatic theme.

Players are tasked with collecting specific cards and achieving objectives while navigating through various scenarios. Each round, players must carefully play their cards, communicate subtly, and strategize without revealing too much information, as they seek to win tricks that align with their mission goals.
