Brass Birmingham

Brass: Birmingham is a strategic, economic board game set during the Industrial Revolution in England. Players take on the role of industrialists, developing networks of canals and railways while building industries like coal, iron, cotton mills, and breweries. The game is played in two phases: the Canal Era and the Rail Era.

Players earn money and victory points by constructing industries, expanding their network, and meeting demand for goods. Resources such as coal and iron must be carefully managed, as they are needed for building and transporting goods.
