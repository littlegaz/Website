Katamino

Katamino is a spatial reasoning puzzle game that challenges players to fill a grid with uniquely shaped wooden pieces. The game features a set of pentominoes (shapes made up of five connected squares) and various board configurations, allowing for multiple levels of difficulty.

Players take turns selecting pieces and placing them on the board to fit perfectly into designated spaces while following specific rules. The objective is to fill the grid completely, creating a seamless arrangement of pieces.

With its simple rules and escalating complexity, Katamino offers a blend of strategy and spatial awareness, making it suitable for players of all ages. The game is both competitive and cooperative, providing a fun way to develop problem-solving skills while enjoying the challenge of fitting the pieces together.
