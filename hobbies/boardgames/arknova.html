Ark Nova

Ark Nova is a strategic board game where players design and manage their own modern zoo. The goal is to build a successful zoo by balancing animal care, conservation projects, and visitor attractions. Players use cards representing animals, enclosures, specialists, and various actions to expand their zoo and enhance its appeal.

The game offers multiple paths to victory, with a focus on building enclosures for animals, partnering with conservation organizations, and increasing scientific research. The action-selection mechanism, where the strength of actions depends on their position in a shifting lineup, adds a unique layer of strategy.

Victory is achieved by earning points through successful zoo management and conservation efforts, making Ark Nova a deep and rewarding game with a strong ecological theme.
