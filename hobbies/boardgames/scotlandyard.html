Scotland Yard

Scotland Yard is a classic deduction board game where players take on the roles of detectives trying to track down Mr. X, a mysterious criminal navigating the streets of London. One player assumes the role of Mr. X, while the others play as detectives working together to corner and capture him.

The game takes place on a map of London, with various transportation options, including buses, taxis, and the underground. Mr. X moves secretly, using tickets for movement, while detectives strategize to predict his path and block his escape routes.

Each turn, detectives share information and make decisions about where to move, while Mr. X tries to outwit them and evade capture. The game combines elements of strategy, teamwork, and deduction, creating an engaging experience filled with tension and excitement. With its blend of cooperation and competition, Scotland Yard is a thrilling game that appeals to players of all ages.
