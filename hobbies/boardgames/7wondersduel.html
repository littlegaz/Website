7 Wonders Duel

7 Wonders Duel is a two-player version of the popular game 7 Wonders, designed specifically for head-to-head competition. Like the original, players take turns developing a civilization over three ages, drafting cards to build structures, gather resources, advance scientific knowledge, strengthen their military, or construct their wonder.

Unlike the original, the card drafting mechanism in 7 Wonders Duel involves a pyramid-like layout, where some cards are face up and others are hidden, only revealed when certain cards are removed. Players must carefully choose their cards, balancing between gaining resources and preventing their opponent from acquiring powerful cards.

Victory can be achieved in three ways: military supremacy (by advancing on a military track), scientific supremacy (by collecting six different science symbols), or by accumulating the most points by the end of the game. The game is known for its deep strategy, direct competition, and tension-filled decision-making.
