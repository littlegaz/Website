7 Wonders

7 Wonders is a popular card drafting and civilization-building board game where players develop one of the seven great ancient wonders. The game spans three ages (rounds), during which players draft cards to build structures, develop their military, advance science, gather resources, and construct their wonder. Each card represents a specific building or technology, and different strategies can lead to victory, such as military dominance, scientific advancements, or building economic power.

Players compete to accumulate the most points by the end of the game, with points being awarded for military victories, completed wonders, scientific achievements, and more. The game is known for its fast-paced play, strategic depth, and simultaneous turns, making it engaging for both casual and seasoned gamers.
